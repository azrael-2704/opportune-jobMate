# Job Opening: Information Security Analyst

## Role Summary
We're looking for a security analyst to help defend our systems and users. This is a hybrid role requiring strong technical and investigative skills.

## Required Skills:
- SIEM tools (Splunk, Sentinel)
- Incident response procedures
- OS hardening (Linux/Windows)
- Firewalls, IDS/IPS

## Responsibilities:
- Monitor real-time security alerts and act on incidents.
- Conduct vulnerability assessments and patch audits.
- Train employees on security best practices.

## Nice to Have:
- Any security certifications (e.g. Security+, CEH)
- Experience with scripting for automation (Python, Bash)

You will report directly to our InfoSec Manager and work closely with DevOps and Infra teams.
